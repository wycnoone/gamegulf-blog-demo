## What kind of game is Blue Prince, really?

**Blue Prince** is not just “adventure, puzzle” in a generic sense. It is a **room-drafting mansion mystery** where each closed door lets you choose what kind of chamber appears next, the house resets each day, and the long-term hook is pushing deeper toward **Room 46** while keeping any permanent estate upgrades you uncover.

That structure matters more than the tag list. The brief specifically calls out **shifting rooms**, **tools and items that combine in different ways**, and a background thread involving **blackmail, political intrigue, and a missing children’s author**. If you like puzzle games that reward note-taking, route planning, and repeated runs through a changing layout, this is a much better fit than the usual one-and-done logic puzzler.
