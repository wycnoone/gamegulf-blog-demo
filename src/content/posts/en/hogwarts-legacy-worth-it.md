## What kind of game is Hogwarts Legacy, really?

**Hogwarts Legacy is a third-person open-world action RPG that sells the fantasy of attending Hogwarts more than reinventing the genre's homework.**

1. **Combat is spell-first.** You rotate damage, control, and stealth-ish tools, upgrade a talent web, and chase gear scores without turning into a pure shooter.
2. **The map is the main character.** Expect hamlets, beast dens, Merlin trials, and dungeon chambers that reward checklist players more than speedrunners.
3. **Switch is the compromise SKU.** If you already watched premium-console footage, recalibrate expectations before you buy — the systems are largely intact, the spectacle is not.

If you dislike Harry Potter theming or bloated open worlds, no amount of polish redeems the pitch — but if you want a cozy-power-fantasy vacation, the loop is honest.
