## What kind of game is Super Mario Party Jamboree, really?

**It is a board-driven party game first, a motion minigame anthology second, and a competitive online experiment third.** Nintendo's pitch still lands: pick characters, roll dice, chase stars, survive minigame breaks, then branch into **Koopathlon** (large online score races), **Bowser Kaboom Squad** (co-op boss raids), or quieter solo progression if you want unlocks without a full couch.

1. **Boards are the spine.** Rainbow Galleria, Goomba Lagoon, and the returning classics give different risk profiles — some lean chaos, some lean planning.
2. **Minigames are the volume play.** **110+** events mean you will find duds, but you will also find keepers your group requests every session.
3. **Online is finally a headline feature.** Twenty-player **Koopathlon** and eight-player **Bowser Kaboom Squad** are the reasons to care if you do not have three friends on the sofa every week.

If you dislike luck swings, buddy RNG, or occasional motion-gated challenges, no Metascore fixes that — buy a different genre.

**Super Mario Party Jamboree targets stable 30fps-style board pacing on Nintendo Switch hardware — this is not a 4K showcase, and it does not need to be.**
