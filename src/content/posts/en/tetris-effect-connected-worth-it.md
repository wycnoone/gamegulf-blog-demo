## What kind of game is Tetris® Effect: Connected, really?

**Tetris® Effect: Connected is classic Tetris rebuilt as a sensory performance, with a short Journey campaign and a deep pile of modes for replay and multiplayer.** The rules are familiar, but the feel is not.

1. **Journey mode is the on-ramp.** You move through 30+ stages that evolve visually and musically as you play, making a puzzle campaign feel strangely narrative without needing cutscenes.
2. **Zone is the real twist.** Zone pauses the fall of Tetriminos so you can stack, breathe, and then cash out huge clears. It's both a bailout tool and a high-score weapon.
3. **Connected is not an afterthought.** You get co-op and competitive multiplayer options that turn it into a party-friendly skill game, not just a solo score chase.

If you love flow states, you will understand the hype in the first 10 minutes. If you only want "a Tetris app," the premium price will feel unnecessary.

## How does Tetris® Effect: Connected run on Switch?

**Tetris® Effect: Connected generally plays at 60 fps on Nintendo Switch, which is exactly what you want for a precision puzzle game.** Some reviews note brief dips during the most effects-heavy moments, but play remains readable and responsive.

- **Handheld fits the game well**: short runs, instant restarts, easy "one more" loops
- **Online is best with friends** if you want consistent matches and co-op sessions
