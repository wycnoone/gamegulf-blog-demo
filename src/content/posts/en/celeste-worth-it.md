## What kind of game is Celeste, really?

**Celeste** is **Matt Makes Games / Extremely OK Games** delivering **screen-by-screen** challenge design: each **room** is a **puzzle-fight** where **dash angles**, **climb stamina**, and **moving hazards** form readable patterns. Compared with **Hollow Knight**’s **metroidvania** map or **Ori**’s **ability-gated** exploration, **Celeste** keeps **linear chapter routes** and escalates **execution** instead of **map gating**.

1. **Madeline’s kit** stays **jump / air-dash / climb** — mastery is about **routing** and **lenient-but-tight** timing, not unlock trees.
2. **Assist Mode** exposes **game-speed**, **stamina**, and **invulnerability** toggles so **narrative-first** players can still summit while **purists** ignore it.
3. **Strawberries, cassette tapes, and crystal hearts** feed optional **B/C-side** gauntlets and **golden berry** runs that **common player reports** describe as **significantly harder** than the **A-side** critical path.
