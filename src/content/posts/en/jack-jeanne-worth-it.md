## What kind of game is Jack Jeanne, really?

Jack Jeanne is an **otome visual novel with rhythm game and time management elements** set in a prestigious all-male theater school. You play as Kisa Tachibana, a girl who disguises herself as a boy to pursue her acting dreams.

**Three core pillars define the experience:**

1. **Visual Novel Storytelling** — 40-60 hours of narrative focused on Kisa's personal growth and relationships with seven male classmates
2. **Rhythm Gameplay** — Singing and dancing mini-games that tie into the theater performance mechanics
3. **Time Management** — Balance classes, rehearsals, and relationship building across a full school year

The game stands out in the otome genre by centering Kisa's ambition rather than pure romance. Every event revolves around her journey to become the lead in the final performance while keeping her identity secret. The art style is distinctive—less polished than Idea Factory titles but with a charming aesthetic that grows on you.
